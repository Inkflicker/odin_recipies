Ultimate Rice and Beans

Description

This is a hearty rice and beans recipie that can use up a lot of leftover vegatables and serve a large group for a low price. Smoky flavours from cumin and paprika create a warm, wholesome meal that can be as spicy as you want.

Ingredients

  • One can of black beans
  • One can of red kidney beans
  • Cumin
  • Smoked paprika
  • Two red onions
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • One teaspoon of honey
  • A splash of balsamic vinegar
  • A jar of jalape√±o peppers
  • Hot sauce
  • One red pepper
  • One cup of rice
  • A tube of tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Vegatable stock
  • A stick of butter

Steps

  1. Wash a cup of rice and then add to a rice cooker
  2. Chop up the onions, red pepper, and about 12 jalapeno slices
  3. Add a lot of olive oil to the pan on a medium heat
  4. After a couple of minutes add the onions and peppers to the pan
  5. Heavily season the pan with cumin and smoked paprika
  6. Cook until the red pepper starts to soften
  7. While it is cooking chop up the garlic
  8. When the red pepper is soft, add the garlic along with a splash of vinegar
  9. Cook for a few minutes
  10. Add both cans of beans after emptying out some, but not all, of the water
  11. Bring the beans to a simmer
  12. While the mixture is simmering and a teaspoon of honey, a squirt of tomato paste, and a teaspoon of vegetable stock
  13. Add salt to taste
  14. After about 30 minutes, when the mixture has boiled down, add a couple chunks of butter
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Add more cumin and paprika to taste
  17. Mix the rice and beans
  18. Serve with guacamole, hot sauce, and sour cream
