Toast and Marmite

Description

A quick and easy breakfast to start the day

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Marmite
  • Two eggs
  1. Put the bread in the toaster
  2. Heat some butter in a frying pan
  3. Crack two eggs into the pan
  4. Turn over the eggs when almost cooked
  5. Take the toast out of the toaster when brown
  6. Butter the toast then add marmite
  7. Put the eggs on top of the toast
