Pasta and Tomato Sauce

Description

A simple, quick, and effective pasta recipie

Ingredients

  • Two white onions
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • 280 grams of dried pasta
  • One can of chopped tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and garlic while heating olive oil in the pan
  2. Add the onions to the pan
  3. After a couple of minutes, add the garlic
  4. Add the can of chopped tomatoes
  5. While the sauce is cooking, boil a large pan of salted water and add the pasta
  6. Add a chunk of butter two the sauce and salt to taste
  7. Add some of the pasta water to the sauce
  8. Strain the pasta and mix in with the sauce before serving
